**Bills (14M):** This category leads the chart with the highest revenue. The significant contribution implies that bill-related transactions are a major revenue driver, likely due to their recurring and essential nature.

**Entertainment (10M):** Following closely, the entertainment category shows strong performance. This suggests that consumer spending in leisure activities remains robust and could be an area of focus for sustained revenue growth.

**Fuel and Grocery (9M each):** Both categories contribute equally to the revenue. Their parity might highlight stable patterns in consumer behavior, reflecting consistent needs for both daily essentials and mobility.

**Travel (6M):** This category records the lowest revenue, which might reflect its discretionary nature. Travel spending is often influenced by seasonal trends and broader economic conditions.
